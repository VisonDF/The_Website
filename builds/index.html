Designed for engineers who need predictable performance, explicit execution, and full control over data pipelines.

At its core, VisonDF is a C++ execution engine that exposes explicit control over evaluation, memory, and parallelism. It combines a hybrid lazy–eager execution model with full user control over execution cost and memory behavior. Every operation is designed to scale: multithreaded by default, vectorized across SSE, AVX2, and AVX-512 when available, and extensible down to the kernel level.

Nothing is hidden, nothing is forced ⇒ you decide what runs, when it runs, and what it costs.
